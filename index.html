Common Radiotherapy Signs & Symptoms

Symptom / Sign Recommended Treatment / Drugs
Radiation Dermatitis (Skin changes) Topical Corticosteroids (e.g., Mometasone), Aloe Vera, Hydrogel
Nausea & Vomiting Ondansetron, Granisetron, Metoclopramide, Dexamethasone
Pain / Mucositis (Oral/GI) Benzydamine mouthwash, Paracetamol, Ibuprofen, Oral morphine
Diarrhea (Pelvic RT) Loperamide, Octreotide (severe cases)
Fatigue Rest, Hydration (Psychostimulants like Methylphenidate rarely)
Cerebral Edema (Brain RT) Dexamethasone (steroids)
